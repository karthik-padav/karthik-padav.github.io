Privacy policy

If you are using our Online Payment system then you will need to provide your credit card type, number, expiration date and billing address for the card or require any information if you paying via any other mode e.g. Net Banking / Debit Card etc. we also require the date of the event (For Confirmation), Name, address and phone number of the client for the purpose of Billing.

You also may be required to create an account on PayUmoney at any time by providing your email address and designating a unique access password. You do not need to create an account in order to visit our site but will need an account for using the Online Payment services. Creating an account allows you to save certain billing information so that you can set your ordering preferences. For your privacy and security, we do not store credit card numbers or any sensitive details in your account.

Cookies:
Talen’s Photography uses session cookies to observe your navigation throughout the website for the purposes described above. You may set your Web browser to notify you when you receive a cookie and you may also erase cookies from your browser after visiting. If you delete our cookies, you limit our ability to provide faster browsing experience (Using Browser cache); however, deleting a cookie will not prevent you from using any other feature on our site in the future.
